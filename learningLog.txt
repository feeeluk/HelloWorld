## OOP

## Namespace
  A Namespace is a collection of classes

## Classes
  Core to OOP
  A class can have two types of things assigned to it.
  1. Attributes (such as variables or constants)
  2. Behaviours (such as methods)

  A good example would be a car.
  A car can have attributes such as:
  - colour


## Objects
  An object is 1 instance of a class

## Instantiation
